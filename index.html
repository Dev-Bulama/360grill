Our Top-rated recipes

Joll of Rice

In every grain of rice, you'll taste the rich heritage of West Africa, where this beloved dish originates. It's a symphony of perfectly cooked rice infused with a medley of spices, vibrant tomatoes, onions, and peppers, creating a tapestry of flavors that dance on your palate.


But Jollof Rice is more than just food; it's a celebration of community, shared memories, and joyous occasions. It's the centerpiece of gatherings, from family feasts to festive occasions, where laughter fills the air and bonds are strengthened over heaping plates of this iconic dish.


At our lounge, we honor the tradition of Jollof Rice while adding our own twist, ensuring each spoonful is an unforgettable experience. Served with tender chicken, succulent shrimp, or a variety of vegetables, it's a journey for your taste buds that you won't soon forget.

Our Top-rated recipes

Suya Chicken

Picture this: succulent, tender chicken, marinated in a symphony of African spices, grilled to perfection over an open flame, creating an explosion of taste with every bite. Savor the rich blend of peanut, garlic, and chili, perfectly balanced to ignite your taste buds without overwhelming them.


Drizzled with a zesty lemon-lime sauce, this dish offers a tantalizing fusion of flavors that dance on your palate. Accompanied by a refreshing side salad and warm, fluffy rice, it's a culinary journey you won't soon forget. Come experience the essence of African cuisine, elevated to new heights, right here at our lounge. Your taste buds will thank you later!

Asun
