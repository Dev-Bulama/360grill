Privacy Policy for 360-Grill Newsletter

Effective Date: November 3, 2025

360-Grill ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when subscribing to our newsletter via our website pop-up forms.
